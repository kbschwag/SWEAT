More About Us

We are three student athletes that have all come over ACL surgeries, been through the college athletic environment, and want to take a stand and talk more about the mental toll it takes on you. This is something that is not talked about a lot and needs to be talked about more, especially with recent suicides that have occurred. Student athletes should be put first more and there needs to be healthy balance between sports, school, AND our mental health, without penalty.

PEOPLE LIKE YOU

Class Year

Talk with people of your same class: Freshman, Sophomore, Junior, Senior, Graduated. We know how hard it is to be a Freshman and feel looked down upon, we also know the struggles of it being your last year as a Senior.
Minorities(Black students, Females students, LGBTQ+ students, Hispanic students,etc.)

Lots of racism, mistreatment, and racial slurs occur in the college athletic world that is not talked about enough. Connect with others and their experiences, so we can create a safe community and help fight this.
Injured

Being injured is so incredibly hard both mentally and physically and people deal with it in different ways. Talk with other injured athletes and support each other at your lows and bring each other up.
Sport

Different sports go through different types of trauma, whether it's the team atmosphere, the coach, or how hard you are or your teammates are on you. Sometimes it's a lot to handle so let's connect with athletes in your sport.
Major

Your major in school can add additional stress, pressure, and can mentally take a toll on you. Talk with other athletes with your major and build a community here!
Outside Pressure

Outside pressure such as parents, teammates, or coach, can add a great deal of stress weighing you down. See who else is dealing with this and share your experiences.
