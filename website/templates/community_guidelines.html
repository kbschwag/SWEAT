Community Guidelines

By using our website, you agree to abide by our community guidelines and terms of service. We reserve the right to remove any content or ban any member who violates our guidelines or terms.

Be Kind

We really want this to be a safe place for anyone who wants to vent so please respond with good intentions and positivity

No Vulgar Content

We are allowing cussing as it is a space to vent and get frustration out, but excessive cussing and not related vulgar content will be condemned

No Spamming or Advertisement

Please do not post any spam or irrelevant content on our website. This includes unsolicited advertisements, promotions, or links to other websites.

No Private Information Allowed

We advise all members of our community to not share any personal information such as contact details, location, or other sensitive information. It is up to you if yous share a schools name or program.

Be tolerant towards other's viewpoints

We encourage all members of the community to be respectful of others. This includes being respectful of other people's opinions, beliefs, and backgrounds. We do not tolerate any form of hate speech or discrimination.

Have Fun and Help us find a solution

We want to bring awareness to the athletic trauma that student athletes endure. Have ideas? Fill out a form and send us feedback!

These guidelines are designed to create a safe and positive space for students to engage with one another. We encourage all members of our community to follow these rules to ensure that our website remains a valuable resource for all.
